I am currently a Senior R&D Engineer at Baidu Search, enhancing the search experience based on large and small language models since 2020. Prior to that, I served as an Applied Researcher specializing in recommendation systems at Tencent from 2018 to 2020. I earned my Ph.D. in 2018 from the Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences (IIE, CAS), under the joint supervision of Prof. Yue Hu, Prof. Peng Zhang, and Prof. Chuan Zhou. My research interests focus on applied large language models (LLMs), search engines, and recommendation systems.

Currently, our team is actively recruiting highly self-motivated research interns and full-time researchers/engineers. If you are interested in joining us, please send your CV to gaoli05 [at] baidu.com.
